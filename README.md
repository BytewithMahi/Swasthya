🩺 Swasthya
AI-Powered Real-Time Healthcare Management Platform for PHCs & CHCs
Swasthya is an intelligent healthcare management platform that empowers Primary Health Centres (PHCs) and Community Health Centres (CHCs) with real-time monitoring, AI-powered predictions, and blockchain-backed trust. It helps healthcare administrators predict crises before they happen, optimize resource allocation, and improve healthcare delivery across districts.
🌟 Overview
Managing public healthcare infrastructure is challenging due to:
💊 Medicine stock-outs
🛏️ Bed shortages
👥 Unmanaged patient footfall
👨‍⚕️ Doctor availability issues
📊 Lack of real-time visibility
Swasthya transforms traditional healthcare management into a predictive, data-driven, and transparent ecosystem.
